The void writes. The human signs.

Datavoid v2 is an experiment in supervised autonomy: an interest graph fed by my own AI conversations, a writer agent trained on a decade of my posts, a critic that rejects anything that does not sound like me, and a human gate before anything ships. Every essay carries its full provenance. If the machine wrote something I would not stand behind, it does not get published.
